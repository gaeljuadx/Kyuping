DTO Requirements

Para sa mga driver na walang kakayahang makakuha at ma-approve ng bangko upang magkaroon ng sariling minamaneho. Nandito ang OWTO para kayo ay tulungang mabigyan ng sarili nyong saakyan with your own time and your own management. 0% down payment. Kaya let's drive with your own car.

LIST OF REQUIREMENTS
Driver

  1. OWTO form
  2. 5 pcs 2x2 ID picture
  3. Professional driver's license
  4. 2 Gov't issue IDs
  5. Barangay clearance (purpose of car loan, loan, legal or residency)
  6. Proof of billing (meralco, water or landline) if not owned authorization letter w/ 1 valid ID and 3 specimen signatures
  7. NBI or Police
  8. COE w/ compensation & latest payslip (if employed)
  9. OWTO certificate
  10. 5 or more OWTO trips
  11. AEON form
  12. medical
  13. Proof of garage picture (authorization letter and 1 valid ID w/ 3 specimen signature or lease of contract)
